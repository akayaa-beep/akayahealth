AKAYA HEALTH

WHERE HEALTH MEETS ELEGANCE

A SOLUTION TO STOP WORRING ABOUT YOUR UNWELL FAMILY MEMBERS OR LOVED ONES.

We plan to offer a health tag in a form of a ring or some other accessory to track down a person's location from an app and provide significant data about a person's illnesses and its solutions or the medications to be provided and emergency contact numbers and also the person's address just by a QR code on the ring/accessory to the people around that ill person , so that he/she can get help immediately .

How It Works:
QR Code Integration: The QR code on the accessory would be linked to an individual’s comprehensive health profile stored securely in an app or cloud service.
Emergency Scenarios: In case of an emergency, anyone (first responders, passersby, or concerned friends) can scan the QR code using their smartphone.
Instant Access: Once scanned, the QR code provides instant access to essential information:
Medical History: Details about existing medical conditions, allergies, surgeries, and chronic illnesses.
Medications: A list of current medications, dosages, and any specific instructions.
Emergency Contacts: Contact information for family members, close friends, or designated emergency contacts.
Address: The individual’s home address, which can be crucial if the person is disoriented or unable to communicate.
Blood Type: Useful for blood transfusions or other medical procedures.
Advantages:
Rapid Response: First responders can make informed decisions quickly, even if the individual is unconscious or unable to communicate.
Universal Language: QR codes are universally recognizable, making them suitable for international travel.
Dynamic Updates: Changes to the health profile (new medications, allergies, or emergency contacts) can be made instantly without needing a new physical ID.
Discreet Design: Unlike traditional medical alert bracelets, the health tag remains inconspicuous until needed.
